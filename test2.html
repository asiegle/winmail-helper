Year-end Donation Programs

 

Do you have unwanted stuff in your dorm room that you want to get rid of before moving out? Don’t trash it; donate it instead! The University offers two programs to get rid of your items in a responsible way. Last year these programs collected 19,874 lbs. of clothing, 2,558 lbs. of food, 1,100 lbs. of electronics, and 1,064 lbs. of other goods! The best part? By participating you are helping others in need and the planet too! Please spread the word!

 

The Move-out Cleanout (Click here for link to poster)

Sponsored by University Facilities and Services

Held from April 30 through May 20

Clothing and Shoes- Look for large yellow Planet Aid collection boxes located outside of each residential living area.

Unopened Non-perishable Food- Collection barrels will be placed in the main entryway of each residence hall. Fraternity Quad residents can bring their goods to Sig Ep.

The barrels located in Susan B. Anthony are designated to benefit The Food Pantry (formerly the Winter and Summer Share program).

Unopened New Toiletries- Collection boxes will be placed in the main entryway of each residence hall. Fraternity Quad residents can bring their goods to Sig Ep.

The boxes located in Susan B. Anthony are designated to benefit The Food Pantry (formerly the Winter and Summer Share program).

Electronics- Label “Recycle” and bring to the Sage or Gale loading docks located in the back of the building. Please note that removal of personal information is strongly recommended as items are left in an open location until data is wiped cleaned by our recycler. For refrigerators, please label “Broken” or “Not Broken”, empty contents, wipe clean and defrost freezer.

Bikes- Label “Recycle” and bring to the O’Brien Hall Bike Room between 8:30am-5:00pm.

Cinder Blocks- Label “Recycle” and bring to the Sage loading dock located in the back of the building.
